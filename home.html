Inua Dada


Welcome to The Inua Dada Foundation

This is an organisation started in 4 th August 2009

It was an initiative established by Julie Gucheru ,a former journalist, who has great interest in this field.

The Inua Dada Foundation is meant to enable women all over the country and beyond, grow in various ways by providing the required support to them

Here ,women are enabled to learn different diverse practical and technological skills as well, with an aim of getting a source of income of their own.
